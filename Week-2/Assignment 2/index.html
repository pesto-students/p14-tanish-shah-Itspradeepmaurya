Ayodhya, Uttarpradesh

Location

Ayodhya is spiritual and religious city located near Sarayu river at north-eastern part of Uttarpradesh

About

Its a very old city famous for birthplace of Lord Ram

Weather

The weather follows mostly three season summer,monsoon , winter. It receives good rainfall and temperture ranges from 5 degree to 45 degree throughout the year.

Population

The corrent population is approx. 5 lac people.

Sports
