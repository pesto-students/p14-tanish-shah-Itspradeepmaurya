Overview


Cuisine: cookis, coffee, tea and lots of DIY foods.

Great food at its best sitting place.

Lake View Cafe has carved a niche for itself in the hearts of people with its excellent services and a larger than life ambiance. The North Indian, Italian, and Continental culinary delight that the place offers promise to take you to a flavorful ride. Every dish in the premium menu is handpicked by our trained chefs to make sure you crave for more with every bite you take. An addition to the list is the extensive bar menu paired up with a Coffee section that instantly lightens up your mood. The Lake View Cafe is located in Central Mumbai and the nearest landmark is Chinmayanand Ashram.
